What is DOME
Marketplace?

DOME is the European marketplace that provides a unified environment where trust, compliance, and interoperability form the foundation of how digital services are published, compared, and used across Europe.

Trusted, Sovereign Digital Ecosystem

DOME ensures services operate under EU rules for security, data protection, and sovereignty.

Transparency and Comparability

Consistent documentation and pricing clarity enable easy comparison of services.

Interoperability and Federation

Shared standards enable seamless discovery and collaboration.

Fair and Open Market Access

Removes barriers to entry and prevents vendor lock-in.
